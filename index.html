About Me

Hello! I'm Hazdizdul A'la, also known as AL, a dedicated CompTIA Security+ certified professional with a strong passion for securing systems, networks, and digital assets against modern cyber threats.

With a solid foundation in cybersecurity fundamentals, including network security, risk management, and incident response, I focus on implementing effective security controls and best practices to protect IT environments.

I believe in continuous learning and staying up to date with the latest security trends, vulnerabilities, and attack techniques. My approach combines technical knowledge with analytical thinking to identify risks and deliver practical security solutions.
